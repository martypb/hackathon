What is a 'hackathon'?

A hackathon is an event where teams compete to build creative solutions to amaze judges over the course of a single day.

The CommSec hackathon will have one minimum criteria - the implemented idea must be of value to CommSec (i.e. it cannot be a random idea with no relevance to CommSec).

The output must also be a working prototype or demonstrable software where software code was developed during the hackathon (it cannot be a powerpoint or a video).

How does it work?

At the start of the event, people with ideas on what to build will pitch their idea or concept and teams will be formed (you can form a team on the day or beforehand).

Then the competition will run for 24-hours, likely to start late afternoon on a Thursday and run till 5pm the following day (to be confirmed).

Presentation by each team of what has been made will be followed by selection of a winner and celebration thereafter. The judging panel will comprise of 3-5 individuals.

How do i get involved?

This event is open to everyone in CommSec, not just IT staff. To build a great solution in just 24 hours we need great collaboration between everyone!

Spread the word in your teams. Some of the best outcomes are likely to be through collaboration between a business representative working with a developer and designer.

We are expecting to hold this event in the first part of September.
