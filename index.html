Hackathon?

Don't worry - a hackathon is an event to build amazing products over the course of a single day.

The CommSec hackathon will have one minimum criteria - the implemented idea must be of value to CommSec (i.e. it cannot be a random idea with no relevance to CommSec).

The minimum standard is that the output is a working prototype or demonstrable software where software code was developed during the hackathon (it cannot be a powerpoint or a video).

Tell me more...

It will run for 24-hours (likely to start late afternoon on a Thursday and run till 5pm the following day.

Presentation of the software will commence around then followed by selection of a winner and celebration thereafter). The judging panel will comprise of 3-5 individuals.

Joe Edwin will sponsor this event and be a judge. Dom Ziino will be another judge. Joe will reach out to a couple of others to form the panel (your ideas/suggestions are welcome).

Who can get involved?

This event is open to everyone in CommSec, not just IT staff. - to build a great product in just 24 hours we need great collaboration between everyone!

How can you help?

Spread the word in your teams. Some of the best outcomes are likely to be through collaboration between a business representative working with a developer and designer.
